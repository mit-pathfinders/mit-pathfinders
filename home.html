Virtual Meetup for High School Students
Network, Interact, and Write Papers
November 28th to December 7th

Learn More Register


MIT Pathfinders is a joint event with the MIT Vaccine Conference that permits high school students to interact with each other to learn and write about vaccination as it relates to the coronavirus pandemic.

What

A virtual meetup to support the digital pandemic response where students can learn more about vaccination and vital information regarding the pandemic!

Who

High school students aged 13+

Where

100% Virtual on the MIT Pathfinders Slack

When

November 28th to December 7th

Essay Information

Learn more about the essay

Prompt

Write an essay discussing vaccination as it relates to the COVID-19 pandemic. Consider vaccine development and/or distribution. This can include implications, problems, comparisons, etc. of various vaccines. You may include examples of vaccines that are currently being developed as well as look ahead to future pandemics. Also, consider placing vaccination in the broader scopes of fighting the pandemic or advancements in technology. Use your imagination backed with research and think outside the box!

Guidelines

Plan on submitting an essay following these guidelines:

  • 1500 words max: excluding abstract and referances
  • Abstract/Claim (300-500 words)
  • Intro/Background
  • Methods
  • Discussions
  • References Page (MLA format)
