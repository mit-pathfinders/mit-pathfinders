Essays for

MIT Vaccines for All Conference

December 11th, 2020 | 1:00 PM EST - 2:00 PM EST
Learn More Register


The Call for Essays at the MIT Vaccine Conference is an exciting opportunity to write, present, and discuss essays about vaccination in the coronavirus pandemic.

Call for Essays

Please pre-register your essay at pathfinders.mit.edu/preregister

Suggested Topics

Write an essay discussing vaccination as it relates to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Consider incorporating these topics (in terms of COVID-19 vaccine distribution) while writing your essay:

  • Logistics: Equity, Consequences
  • Health Outcomes: Efficiency, Variations
  • User-Centric Issues: Data Privacy, Dosage Tracking, Vaccine Incentivization and Motivation
  • Communication: Political Communication, Misinformation, Trust

Submit your essay at pathfinders.mit.edu/submit.

Guidelines
  1. Your essay must not exceed 1500 words (excluding Abstract and References)

  2. Essays must use 12 pt font size and the Times New Roman font.

  3. Your essay must follow the below outline:
    • Abstract/Claim (300-500 words)
    • Intro/Background
    • Methods
    • Discussions
    • References (MLA format)
